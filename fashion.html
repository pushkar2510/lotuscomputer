What is Fashion Designing?
Fashion Designing is a dynamic and creative field that involves the art of creating clothing and accessories. It combines various skills such as sketching, fabric selection, pattern making, and understanding fashion trends. Our course at Lotus Computers is designed to provide you with the skills needed to excel in the fashion industry.

Why Choose Fashion Designing?

  • Creative Expression: Fashion designing allows you to express creativity through the medium of clothing and accessories.
  • Career Opportunities: A career in fashion can open doors to various opportunities in design, merchandising, and retail.
  • Practical Learning: Hands-on training with real-world projects, fabric knowledge, and pattern making.
  • Industry-Relevant Skills: Learn the latest trends and techniques used by professionals in the fashion industry.


What You Will Learn

  • Fabric Selection and Textiles
  • Pattern Making and Draping
  • Blouse Making
